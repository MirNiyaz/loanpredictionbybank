Bank Loan Prediction?

Bank Loan?

Loans are the core business of banks. The main profit comes directly from the loan’s interest. The loan companies grant a loan after an intensive process of verification and validation. However, they still don’t have assurance if the applicant is able to repay the loan with no difficulties. In this tutorial, we’ll build a predictive model to predict if an applicant is Eligible To get The Loan or not. We will prepare the data using Jupyter Notebook and use various models to predict the target variable.
